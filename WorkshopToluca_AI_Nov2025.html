Ing. Leidy Mora

Universidad Militar Nueva Granada

Leidy Mora is a biomedical engineer who graduated from the Nueva Granada Military University. She is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Biomedical Engineering, focusing her research on the application of artificial intelligence to echocardiography. She has experience in research projects that include the use of ultrasound images as a guide for medical procedures, as well as in the three-dimensional reconstruction of anatomical structures using 3D Slicer software. Her interest lies in the development of innovative technologies to improve health diagnosis and treatment.
