3D Slicer for Latin America Outreach Events

Dr. Sonia Pujol's IEEE EMBS Invited Talk, June 13, 2026, Montevideo, Uruguay

Dr. Sonia Pujol delivered an invited keynote lecture at the IEEE EMBS Student Chapter, Universidad de la República (UdelaR), Montevideo, Uruguay, on June 13, 2026. Her presentation focused on 3D Slicer as a leading open-source platform for medical imaging and 3D digital modeling, as part of the Introduction to 3D Engineering in Medicine program. This marks the first official activity of the Slicer for Latin America project in Uruguay.

38th Slicer Project Week hackathon (PW38), Jan.30 - Feb.3, 2023, Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain

The 38th Slicer Project Week was held January 30 - February 3, 2023, in Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain. Dr. Sonia Pujol hosted daily online sessions on 3D Slicer internationalization and introduced the "3D Slicer for Latin America: Localization and Outreach" project to the 3D Slicer community at the event. Dr. Adriana Vilchis González and Dr. Luiz Murta participated in the daily sessions that brought together 23 participants from Mexico, Brazil, Senegal, Hungary, Mauritania, the USA, and Canada. Dr. Pujol gave a training tutorial on how to use 3D Slicer internationalization infrastructure to translate 3D Slicer in multiple languages at the event. The Latin American team members contributed to the 3D Slicer Internationalization project.
