Slicer Toluca Workshop, Oct.8 & 10, 2025, Toluca, Mexico

The Slicer Toluca Workshop, held on October 8 & 10, 2025, in Toluca, Mexico, focused on AI-based technology for Rehabilitation Medicine Physicians.

Slicer Ribeirão Preto Workshop, June 30, 2025, Ribeirão Preto, Brazil

Slicer Toluca Workshop, June 6, 2025, Toluca, Mexico

42nd Slicer Project Week hackathon (PW42), Jan.27-31, 2025, Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain

The 42nd NA-MIC Project Week was held in person at Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain. Douglas Samuel Gonçalves, Lucas Sanchez Silva, and Victor Manuel Montaño Serrano attended in person. The team worked on the 3D Slicer for Latin America project, advancing Tutorial Maker with bug fixes, new features, automated testing infrastructure, and translations of tutorials to Portuguese and Spanish.

CZI Open Science 2024

Sonia Pujol presented the 3D Slicer for Latin America project at the Open Science in Latin America session of the 2024 CZI Open Science annual meeting.

Brazilian Congress of Biomedical Engineering (CBEB2024), September 2-6, 2024, Ribeirão Preto, Brazil

The Slicer for Latin America team presented a poster entitled "Preliminary Results of 3D Slicer Localization to Portuguese and Spanish for the Latin American Biomedical Engineering Community" at the XIX Brazilian Congress of Biomedical Engineering (CBEB2024), September 2-6, 2024, Ribeirão Preto, Brazil.

41st Slicer Project Week hackathon (PW41), June 24-28, 2024, Cambridge, MA, USA

The 41st NA-MIC Project Week was held June 24–28, 2024, at MIT, Cambridge, MA. The Tutorial Maker module was showcased at the 3D Slicer for Latin America project and is being tested by the community.

Slicer Toluca Workshop, June 10, 2024, Toluca, Mexico

The Slicer Toluca Workshop, held on June 10, 2024, in Toluca, Mexico, focused on Segmentation and 3D Reconstruction.

Slicer Toluca Workshop, May 7 & 14, 2024, Toluca, Mexico

The Slicer Toluca Workshop, held on May 7 & 14, 2024, in Toluca, Mexico, focused on Obtaining 3D models of the kidneys from CT imaging.

40th Slicer Project Week hackathon (PW40), Jan.29 - Feb.2, 2024, Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain

The 40th NA-MIC Project Week was held January 29 – February 2, 2024, at Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain, and was the first in-person event for the Latin American team, who contributed to the 3D Slicer for Latin America project.

Three-day 3D Slicer Hackathon, Oct.8-10, 2023, Ribeirão Preto, Brazil

The Brazilian team organized a three-day 3D Slicer Hackathon within the XI Symposium on Medical Imaging and Instrumentation program held from October 8 to 10, 2023, at the University of São Paulo, Ribeirão Preto campus.

39th Slicer Project Week hackathon (PW39), June 12-16, 2023, Montreal, Canada

The 39th NA-MIC Project Week was held June 12–16, 2023, at École de Technologie Supérieure, Montreal, Canada. The team participated with the 3D Slicer for Latin America project, advancing Tutorial Maker development and Slicer localization.

38th Slicer Project Week hackathon (PW38), Jan.30 - Feb.3, 2023, Gran Canaria, Spain

The 38th NA-MIC Project Week was held January 30 – February 3, 2023, in Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain. The Latin American team members contributed to the 3D Slicer Internationalization project.
