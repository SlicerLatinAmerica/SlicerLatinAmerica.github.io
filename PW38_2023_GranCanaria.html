Location: Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain

Description

The 38th NA-MIC Project Week was a hybrid event held in person at Hotel Cristina, Las Palmas, Gran Canaria, Spain, from January 30 to February 3, 2023. It was the first in-person Project Week since 2020.

The Latin American and African teams contributed to the 3D Slicer Internationalization project, working on localizing 3D Slicer into Spanish, Portuguese, French, Arabic, and other languages. The team included members from Mexico, Brazil, Senegal, and Mauritania, most of whom participated online.
